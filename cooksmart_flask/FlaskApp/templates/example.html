I happen to like the "modals" that Bootstrap offers. These are where you click on a link and, instead of loading to another page, the background dims out a bit and there is a sort of "pop up" window. This seems to fit fairly well with something like a log in button, but can also fit for a sort of "more information" button. Obviously there are many reasons why this might work, however.
